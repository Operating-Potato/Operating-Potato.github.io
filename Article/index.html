Tetra

The word tetra describes various species of small fish in the order Characiformes. Tetras include many species commonly kept in aquaria, such as neon tetras (pictured), phantom tetras, emperor tetras, ornate tetras, diamond tetras, and ornate tetras in the Characidae family, African tetras such as Congo tetras in the family Alestidae, and pencilfishes (Lebiasinidae).
